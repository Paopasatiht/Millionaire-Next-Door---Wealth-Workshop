# Question Bank

Diagnostic questions drawn from *The Millionaire Next Door*. Use during annual review and monthly check-ins.

---

## Core 4 Questions (Chapter 1–2)

| # | Question | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Do you operate on a written annual budget? | Annual setup |
| 2 | Do you know how much your family spends on food, clothing, and shelter annually? | Annual setup + monthly log |
| 3 | Do you have clearly defined goals across all time horizons? | Annual setup + quarterly review |
| 4 | How many hours per month do you spend planning your financial future? | Monthly log |

---

## Self-Assessment Questions (Annual Review)

### Wealth Accumulation
- What is my current net worth?
- What is my expected net worth (Age × Income / 10)?
- Am I a PAW, AAW, or UAW?
- What changed my ratio this year?

### Income vs Lifestyle
- Did my lifestyle expenses grow faster than my income this year?
- What was my savings rate this year? (target: ≥ 20%)
- Which expense categories surprised me most?

### Goals & Planning
- Did I complete my annual financial goals?
- Which goals moved from annual → lifetime?
- Did I average 8+ hours/month on financial planning?

---

## Monthly Check-in Questions (~5 min)

1. Did I stay within budget this month?
2. Which category overspent the most? Why?
3. Did I log my planning hours?
4. Is there any goal I need to adjust this month?

---

## Quarterly Review Questions (~30 min)

1. Am I on track for my annual savings goal?
2. Have any of my annual goals become irrelevant? Should I replace them?
3. What have I learned about investing this quarter?
4. What habit is most helping/hurting my wealth building?
